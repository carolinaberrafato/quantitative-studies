# Summary

Here you'll find my notes and assignments' answers related to the course **Introduction to Mathematical Thinking**, offered by Stanford University via Coursera and taught by professor Keith Devlin. Course information can be found [on its Coursera's official page](https://www.coursera.org/learn/mathematical-thinking).

## What am I learning about?

The course's goal is to train the learners to think mathematically: instead of teaching tools and/or procedures, the course **focus on the thinking process to solve problems using mathematics**. Topics covered include concepts of propositional logic and mathematical proofs, but differently from most courses, the approach taken prioritizes mastering the thinking over finding the right answer.
